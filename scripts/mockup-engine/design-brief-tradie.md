## The site

A premium, conversion-focused **one-page website for a local trade business**.
Example data (replace per client):

- Business: **Western Sydney Plumbing**
- Trade: **Plumber**
- Area: **Penrith & surrounding Sydney suburbs**
- Google rating: **4.7** from **92 reviews**
- Phone: **0412 345 678**

## Audience & goal

The visitor is a **non-technical homeowner** with a plumbing problem. The only
goals: **call now** or **request a quote**. Everything serves those two actions.

## Sections (top to bottom)

1. **Header** (sticky): business name · nav (Services, Why Us, Reviews, Contact) ·
   phone number · a "Get a Quote" button.
2. **Hero** (asymmetric, editorial — NOT centered, NOT left-text/right-image
   skeleton): a large display headline ("Reliable plumbing, done right.") with
   one accent-coloured word · a one-line subheadline · "Get a Quote" + "Call now"
   buttons · a tall **real photograph of finished work / a modern bathroom**, with
   a small floating card showing the 4.7 Google rating + stars.
3. **Marquee**: thin strip of keywords (PLUMBING · PENRITH · FAST QUOTES · …).
4. **Services**: 6 services — Emergency Repairs, Blocked Drains, Hot Water
   Systems, Leak Detection, Bathroom Renovations, Gas Fitting — as a clean
   bordered grid with simple **line icons** and index numbers. No nested cards.
5. **Why us** (split): a **real photo of a tradesperson at work** + three
   checkmark benefits (On-time, every time · Tidy, respectful work · Clear upfront
   pricing) + big accent stat numbers (4.7 rating · 92 reviews).
6. **Reviews**: 2–3 realistic Google review cards (name + stars + short quote).
7. **CTA band**: bold accent band — "Need a plumber in Penrith today?" + call button.
8. **Contact**: a quote-request form (name, phone, what you need) + contact details
   (phone, service area, response time).
9. **Footer**: business name, trade, area, year.

## Style direction (the anti-slop rules)

- **Palette:** deep ink/charcoal `#0C0E12` backgrounds, one warm **copper** accent
  `#E2733B`, cream `#F4F1EA`. **NO purple, indigo, violet, or default blue.**
- **Type:** large editorial display headlines (Bricolage Grotesque), Inter body,
  strong scale contrast, tight tracking.
- **Layout:** asymmetric, spacious, generous negative space.
- **Imagery:** tasteful **real photography** of plumbing work / bathrooms /
  tradespeople. **Never fake or AI-looking people. Never clipart. Never emoji icons.**
- **Craft:** subtle motion, hover polish, thin marquee, real-feeling review cards.
